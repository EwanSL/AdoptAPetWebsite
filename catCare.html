7 Tips of Cat Care


Bringing a new kitten home is exciting, but proper care is essential for a long and healthy life. Here are seven key aspects of responsible cat ownership:


1. Proper Feeding and Hydration

Cats require a balanced diet rich in animal-based protein. Provide fresh water at all times and avoid overfeeding.


2. Regular Grooming

Even though cats groom themselves, brushing, nail trimming, and dental care help maintain their health and hygiene.


3. Proper Housing

Give your cat a clean, quiet place to sleep and rest. Provide cat trees or elevated spaces for security.


4. Litter Box Maintenance

Keep the litter box clean and in a quiet area. Provide one litter box per cat plus one extra.


5. Scratching Needs

Scratching is natural behavior. Provide scratching posts or boards to protect furniture.


6. Spaying and Neutering

Spaying/neutering prevents unwanted kittens and reduces health risks and behavioral issues.


7. Maintaining a Healthy Weight

Regular exercise and portion control help prevent obesity, ensuring long-term health.
