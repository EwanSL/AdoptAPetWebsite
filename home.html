~ Welcome to Happy Tails Shelter! ~

At Happy Tails Shelter, we believe every pet deserves a loving home. Our mission is to rescue and rehome cats and dogs in need of care and compassion.


Why Choose Us?

We are a dedicated group committed to helping animals find their forever families. With our extensive adoption process, we ensure that every pet finds the right match based on their needs and the adopter's lifestyle.


Adopt, Don't Shop!

If you're looking for a furry friend to join your family, browse our available pets and find the perfect companion. Every adoption makes a difference!


Help Us Make a Difference

Interested in making a positive impact on animals in need? Learn more about how you can help by donating or volunteering with us. Visit our Contact Us page to get involved.
